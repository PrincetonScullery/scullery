Featured Member: Linh Tran

Linh is an international student from Hanoi, Vietnam who recently took a gap year because she decided that 2020 sounds way better as a class year and for once, those silly number glasses would actually make a bit of sense. You'll find Linh aggressively scrubbing dishes or sharpening knives in the Scully kitchen, not because she is venting her anger but rather because she loves to procrastinate by performing repetitive, mindless tasks. Linh enjoys things that make her feel warm and fuzzy which is anything from a good cup of coffee with milk, a ridiculous quantity of condensed milk and avocado, freshly washed laundry, or a good talk. A worshipper of glutamates & ketchup.
